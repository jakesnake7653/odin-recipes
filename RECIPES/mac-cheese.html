Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Cooked elbow noodles mixed with cheese, with a crispy cheese top.

Ingredients include

  • Elbow Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil and add elbow noodles
  2. Let cook for 10 minutes, strain noodles
  3. Mix cheese and milk, and melt in a pan.
  4. Mix cheese and macaroni and add to a baking pan. Top with more cheese
  5. Set pan inside oven, briol until cheese is crispy
