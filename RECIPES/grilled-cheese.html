Grilled Cheese

Ingredients include

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Take out 2 slices of bread, and apply butter to 1 side of each piece of bread
  2. Slice Cheese into thin slices and set them aside
  3. Take out a pan and set your stove to a meduim heat
  4. Put a piece of bread, butter side down, on the pan. Then add Cheese to the bread and add the other piece of bread with the butter face up.
  5. After 5 minutes flip the grilled cheese
  6. Let cook for another 5 minutes, the take off heat. Done!
